Accessibility As A Professional


As a software developer in a professional setting, accesibility is extremely important to me. It is crucial to provide services that are easily accessible as many people as possible, so that everyone, even people that have special needs, can access and benefit from the product created. It is also important for me to continue the practice of accessible design and lead by example so that other developers can take the same path.
